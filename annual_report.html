What didn't go well

There are a few things I wanted to achieve and didn't get around to doing. It's important to acknowledge these gaps.

On June 2rd(Jeudi, 2 Juin 2022) I scheduled to launch a project of building a nameplate wall, like a hall of fame, to document something important. Apparently the project has sunk to the bottom, partly because of some technical hurdles, and partly because of the risk of privacy leak, and so there's no anything substantial published this year.

Second, I wanted to escape the fence for a trip, probably it's our mutual common wish in this year. We had a cherry blossom spring, a dry and hot summer, and a dreary autumn, but never got our wish accomplished till the end of the year.

Third, we have fallen into the trap of sentimentality when negative messages piled up this year. We've heard tremendous sensational things, we've questioned the impartiality of the media countless times, we've lost the analysis ability to deal with the source in a compartmentalized way. This takes away our position of political neutrality.

Fourth, we have a strong sense of being dominated by life, which makes us lose our autonomy. Sometimes we have no idea on the reasons why we are going to do something. Fortunately, we quickly realized we are in an abnormal state, and that remains a silver lining for us to watch our life from outside as a dissociated observer, a more nuanced perspective to change and persist on living.

In a nutshell, we could summarize all these as endogenous abnormalities due to disturbances in the external environment. We realized this and so we could launch a renaissance.

New year annual strategy

In the passing year we all face an extraordinarily complicated world, filled with a wide range of challenges. Due to some reasons, we feel our lives porous and vulnerable. These things make us have lost trust, faith and confidence in our future.

Nearly one year later, although serious challenges remain, we are surely have to chart a new and very different couse.

We will pursue this beautiful vision: having ability to rationally analyze and evaluate the dilemma we confront at any time, having confidence in the path we choose with absolutely no wavering, thriving side by side with our like-minded partners in enhancement, progress and prosperity. We shall focus more on ourselves and no longer dominated by life, we shall rely more on ourselves rather than illusion that people could help, and we shall all resolutely adhere to a neutrality attitude.

In pursuit of that future, we will look at the world with clear eyes and fresh thinking, we will promote a balance of development that favors us, we will never lose our own belief, criteria and values and capacity of inspire, uplift and renew.

We shall be vigilant that do not bred complacency under no circumstance, self-sustaining won't always work, that spurs us on continuous learning from the external environment and staying humble. History has proved the greatest antidote to bewildered, scorn, arrogance is to do something with our hands, and that's when we will find what a wrong perception we've had.

Competitions and rivalries are inevitable and are not passing trends or momentary problems, they will be there throughout our working life span. They are intertwined, long term challenges that demand our sustained attention, alert and commitment, and here comes our overall annually strategic points:

  • Spotlight, we will refocus, the time we spend in caring about fruitless things will be put into things that do practical benefit for us. Before we wings are full, sparks won't lit the candle.
  • Neutrality, we will not comment on any political affairs or issues and there will be no response for our views.
  • Solidarity, cooperation does good to anyone.
  • Community, the negative recognition has blindfolded us this year. We questioned the necessity in attending community events and now we know it's wrong. In the incoming year, we will restart our voluntary for a shared future.
  • Observation, silence is gold, so does observation. Information we gain from observation will have potential advantages.

This strategy is a layout, a roadmap for the incoming year, instead of wandering, we are wondering. We know none if this will be easy or without setbacks, but that is our enduring vision.
